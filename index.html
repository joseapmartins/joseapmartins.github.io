About

I study the physics of polymers and soft materials. My work focuses on crystallization kinetics (including melt memory), the physical nature of entanglements, rheology and flow activation volumes, and short‑range order in polymer melts. I also build experimental methods and analysis protocols for studying flow‑induced crystallization.

Research

Melt memory & crystallization

Quiescent vs. shear‑induced kinetics; steady‑state shear; spherulite size prediction.

Entanglements — physical basis

From binary contacts to short‑range ordered regions; implications for plateau modulus.

Rheology & flow activation volume

Step‑shear protocols; tube model interpretation; nanocomposites with CNT.

Short‑range order in melts

MD‑informed analyses of local order in polyethylene and other melts.

Instrumentation

Shear DTA for isothermal & non‑isothermal crystallization under controlled shear.
